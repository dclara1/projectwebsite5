About Me

My name is David and I was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona. I am a first generation Mexican American and I am one out of seven siblings. Out of my seven siblings, I was the first in my family to graduate from University, Arizona State University. I have worked many jobs from finance, to self driving cars, to warehouses and I have got a good sense of what career I am looking for now.

I have always been a huge nerd for video games, anime, super heroes, and so much more. I really got into computers once I started to make gaming computers and now I am so interested in software and just how much I can do with a computer. This is why I would like to start my next career in technology. If you want more information, please click HERE to check out my linkedin.

Becoming a Software Developer

I first started my journey in this career path to become a software developer when I was in college and I took an intro to programming class. It taught me a bit of HTML and I really enjoyed their class, but at the time I was more focused on just passing my class over my career. Some years later, I tried to do a free Udemy class to see if I still enjoyed it, but life happened and I did not get the chance to really get into it again. Now, I am at the point in my life that I wanted a more clear career path and started to think about what I enjoy. After considering that I enjoy computers and found coding interesting in the past, I decided to try to take a chance on becoming a software developer.

Since I just got married, I started to really think about my career and I knew I wanted to take advantage of Amazon's Career Choice program. I signed up and started the Amazon Software Development training course with Correlation One. I am currently taking the course and learning how to code. As of right now, I still have a lot to learn, but I am enjoying this learning process and I cannot wait to become a software developer.
